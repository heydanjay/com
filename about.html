About Me

Hey, I'm Danjay. Nice to meet you!

I've always loved computers and technology since I was a kid, getting out first family computer when I was 6, and getting the internet when I was 8. I'm well into my 30's, working as a software dev these day, and remember the wild-west days of the early internet fondly.

Web 2.0 (and I guess 3.0 now?) have slowly ground me down over the years and left me feeling increasingly disenchanted and bored with the internet. I came across the Web Revival and Small Web movements recently, and decided to setup this little corner for myself and try to revive some of that early fun and charm of the net.

Drop me an email, I'd love to here from you and connect to people with similar interests. hey@heydanjay.com
